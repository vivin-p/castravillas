About Us

Castra Villas - Where Luxury Meets the Wild Heart of Thekkady

Nestled atop the stunning Parunthumpara Mountain Point in Idukki, Castra offers breathtaking views and an ideal retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers. With a range of rooms from cozy honeymoon cottages to spacious family villas, each featuring a private balcony with panoramic views, you'll wake up to the beauty of misty mountains and lush valleys. We provide a range of experiences including guided treks, jeep off-road safaris, campfires with music, and BBQ on demand. Enjoy homely meals made from locally sourced ingredients and relax in the serene ambiance of our resort. Whether you're looking for adventure or tranquility, Castra offers the perfect blend of luxury and nature. Book your stay today and immerse yourself in the beauty of Thekkady.
